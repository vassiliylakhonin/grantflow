State Department Media Resilience Implementation Reference (Seed)

Implementation focus areas
- Professional standards in editorial practice and verification.
- Capacity for coordinated response to disinformation campaigns.
- Practical safety and continuity planning for media actors.

Illustrative activity package
1. Editorial verification and fact-checking routines.
2. Legal and digital risk awareness training.
3. Small grants for audience engagement and content integrity pilots.
4. Coordination mechanisms between media and civic support actors.

Measurement direction
Track changes in newsroom practices, coordination frequency, and use of risk mitigation protocols.
