## Confirmed Pilot Success Criteria

The customer accepted the following as the pilot success bar:

- `25%+` reduction in time to first reviewable draft
- at least `1` fewer full review loop
- `20%` reduction in time to final package
- cleaner findings/comments with less duplication and conflict
- fewer late-stage major MEL rewrites
