## KPI Delta Versus Baseline

For each case, compare against locked baseline:
- first reviewable draft time
- review loops
- active reviewers
- MEL rewrite burden

Use simple interpretation:
- ahead of target
- on track
- behind target

## Review Workflow Readout

Summarize:
- whether review chaos decreased
- whether approvals became clearer
- whether MEL/logframe alignment happened earlier
- whether finance/compliance timing worked as intended

## Immediate Decisions

Record:
1. what to keep unchanged for `R2`
2. what to adjust before `R2`
3. whether any donor corpus needs supplementation
4. whether any workflow role is overloaded

## R1 Exit Decision

Choose one:
- continue to `R2`
- continue with conditions
- hold and correct workflow issues
