# External Proof Summary

GrantFlow has bounded pilot proof that the proposal-operations model improves throughput and reviewability for institutional donor workflows.

## What The Proof Shows

- faster movement to a reviewable first draft
- fewer review loops on the strongest donor paths
- cleaner review coordination
- grounded, export-ready draft artifacts

## Where Proof Is Strongest

Current strongest public proof is on:
- `UN agencies`
- `EU`

There is also conditional proof on:
- `USAID`

The honest claim is not that every donor path is equally mature.
The honest claim is that the proposal-operations model is working, with strongest proof on `UN/EU` and conditional proof on `USAID`.

## What This Means Commercially

Large proposal teams do not buy text generation by itself.
They buy:
- faster reviewable drafts
- lower review chaos
- clearer reviewer routing
- cleaner export handoff
- reusable workflow infrastructure
